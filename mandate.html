The Startup Club

About

The Startup Club (TSC) is a non-profit organization established on 30th July 2023 and is headquartered in Chennai, India.

Until September 2024, we had primarily focused on virtual events. You can find a history of our events on our YouTube channel. Starting in October 2024, we have transitioned to organize physical events.

Organizational Mandate

Our overarching objective is to establish a functional support system for members of the startup ecosystem in India. We wish to bring together students, aspiring founders, founders, investors, incubators, service providers, and other stakeholders of the ecosystem together in a constructive and collaborative ecosystem.

We believe that India is at the cusp of an entrepreneurial boom and our goal is to accelerate the evolution of each entrepreneur and aspiring entrepreneur so that they fail faster, gather experiences sooner and are better informed so that they leverage resources to achieve their first success faster.

Moving forward we will be working to establish micro-hubs across India to create physical centers of learning, growth and knowledge exchange. If you wish to collaborate with us, you can find the necessary details to contact us here.
